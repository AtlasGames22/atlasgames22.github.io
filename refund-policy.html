Refund Policy

Refunds may be available for Atlas Games purchases subject to platform policies and the eligibility criteria outlined below. Please review these terms before requesting a refund.

Last updated: August 11, 2025

Overview

Most Atlas Games purchases are processed through third-party platforms such as Itch.io. For these purchases, the platform's refund policy applies first and takes precedence over this policy. Please check with the platform where you made your purchase for their specific refund procedures.

For purchases made directly from Atlas Games (if available), this refund policy applies. We strive to provide fair and reasonable refund options while protecting our business from abuse.

Digital goods are typically non-returnable once downloaded, accessed, or played, as they cannot be "returned" in the traditional sense. However, we recognize that exceptional circumstances may warrant refund consideration under the conditions outlined below.

Refund Eligibility

To be eligible for a refund, your request must meet the following criteria:

  • Time window: Request must be made within 14 days of purchase and with less than 2 hours of playtime
  • Proof of purchase: You must provide valid proof of purchase including order ID, receipt email, or transaction reference
  • Valid reason: Technical issues, accidental purchases, or legitimate dissatisfaction with the product
  • Regional rights: Additional consumer protection rights may apply based on your local jurisdiction
  • Gift purchases: Gifts or product keys obtained from third parties are subject to the original seller's refund policy
  • Payment disputes: Initiating chargebacks or payment disputes may result in license revocation and account restrictions

Non-Refundable Cases

The following situations are generally not eligible for refunds:

  • Substantial use: Games played for 2 hours or more, or completed content/campaigns
  • Discounted purchases: Heavily discounted bundles, charity packs, or promotional items (unless required by law)
  • Time limits exceeded: Requests made more than 14 days after purchase
  • Policy violations: Account suspensions or bans due to violations of our Terms of Service, EULA, or Community Guidelines
  • System compatibility: Issues arising from failure to meet published system requirements
  • Change of mind: Simple preference changes after substantial gameplay or content consumption

  3. Contact us: Submit your request using the form below or email us directly at the address provided
