About Atlas Games

We're an indie game studio dedicated to creating games that stand the test of time. Like Atlas holding up the heavens, we support memorable gaming experiences that endure.

Our Mission

At Atlas Games, we believe games should be more than entertainment—they should be experiences that create lasting memories and bring people together.

Our mission is to craft games that endure beyond trends and technology cycles. We focus on timeless gameplay mechanics, meaningful player interactions, and stories that resonate across generations.

Our Core Values

Quality Craft

Every line of code, every pixel of art, and every sound effect is carefully crafted with attention to detail. We believe quality is never an accident—it's always the result of intelligent effort.

Community First

Our players are at the heart of everything we do. We actively listen to feedback, engage with our community, and create games that bring people together rather than divide them.

Lasting Impact

We design for longevity, creating games that remain engaging years after release. Our goal is to build experiences that players return to again and again, finding new joy each time.

Our Journey

The Beginning (2022)

Atlas Games was founded by three passionate developers who met through their shared love of creating unique gaming experiences. Starting with small experimental projects, we quickly discovered our shared vision for creating games that truly endure.

Our first major success came with the original Wobbly Wiggles, a physics-based party game that captured players' hearts with its simple yet engaging gameplay.

Growth & Evolution (2023-2024)

Building on our early success, we expanded our catalog with diverse games across multiple genres. From the farming simulation of Harvest Hustle to the chaotic action of Kenny's Khaos, we've continuously pushed our creative boundaries.

The Wobbly Wiggles series grew into a beloved franchise, culminating in the racing adventure of Wobbly Wiggles 3: Ultimate Racers.

Looking Forward (2025+)

As we enter 2025, we're excited about our upcoming projects including Timmy's Revenge, From The Shadows, and Cancer Crusher. Each represents our commitment to exploring new themes while maintaining the quality our community expects.

We're also expanding our educational game development with meaningful projects that combine entertainment with important social messages.
