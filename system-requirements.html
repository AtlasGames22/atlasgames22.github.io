System Requirements

Our games support Windows, macOS, and Linux platforms with specifications that vary by title and target performance settings.

Important Notes

  • Specifications are representative for our current catalog and may change with game updates or new releases
  • Laptop GPUs with similar performance levels are generally supported, though may require adjusted settings
  • Stable internet connection required for game updates, leaderboards, and online features where applicable
  • Controller support is optional and varies by title; all games support keyboard and mouse input
  • For crashes, performance issues, or compatibility problems, please visit our Support Center or submit a bug report

Windows Requirements

Our games are tested on Windows 10 and 11 (64-bit) systems with DirectX 11 or newer. Older versions of Windows are not officially supported but may work with reduced compatibility.

Game-Specific Notes

Harvest Hustle

Very light system requirements. Runs smoothly on integrated GPUs at 1080p with low settings. Excellent choice for older hardware.

Kenny's Khaos

Physics-heavy scenes during intense platforming may require mid-tier GPU for consistent 60fps. CPU-intensive during particle effects.

Tiny Cafe

CPU-bound performance during high NPC counts and complex restaurant simulations. Benefits significantly from higher core count processors.

Void Breaker

Benefits greatly from SSD storage for seamless level streaming and reduced loading times. High-speed storage recommended for optimal experience.

Timmy's Revenge

Controller strongly recommended for optimal gameplay experience. Supports Xbox, PlayStation, and generic XInput-compatible controllers.

Honey Haven

Stable performance at 1080p medium settings on entry-level GPUs. Well-optimized for lower-end hardware with scalable graphics options.

Display & Input Support

Display

  • Primary testing at 1920x1080 (1080p) resolution
  • Ultrawide monitor support (21:9 aspect ratio) available
  • 4K gaming supported on recommended hardware specs
  • Windowed, borderless, and fullscreen modes
  • Variable refresh rate (G-Sync/FreeSync) compatibility

Input

  • Keyboard and mouse support (primary input method)
  • XInput-compatible controllers (Xbox, generic)
  • PlayStation controllers with additional drivers
  • Key remapping varies by title (check in-game settings)
  • Steam Input support for custom controller configurations
