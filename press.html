Studio Boilerplate

Atlas Games is an independent game development studio founded in 2022 and based in Durban, South Africa. The studio specializes in creating compact, polished, and highly replayable indie games that prioritize player engagement over scope. With a focus on craftsmanship and community feedback, Atlas Games has built a reputation for delivering quality experiences across PC and web platforms. The team believes in games that endure, creating lasting value for players through thoughtful design and continuous post-launch support.
