Frequently Asked Questions

How can I report a bug in one of your games?

Please email us at atlasgamesstudio22@gmail.com with details about the bug, including what platform you're playing on and steps to reproduce the issue.

Do you accept game ideas or submissions?

While we appreciate your creativity, we don't accept unsolicited game ideas. However, we love hearing about what types of games you'd like to see us make!

Are you hiring?

We're a small team, but we're always interested in connecting with talented developers, artists, and designers. Feel free to reach out with your portfolio!

Can I stream or create content about your games?

Absolutely! We love seeing people enjoy our games. Feel free to stream, record, and create content about any of our games. Just tag us so we can see!

How can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled through the platform where you purchased the game (Steam, itch.io, etc.). Please check their refund policies or contact us if you need assistance.

Do you offer press kits?

Yes! If you're a member of the press or content creator, please email us with information about your publication/channel and we'll send you what you need.
