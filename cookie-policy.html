What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember information about your visit, such as your preferred language and other settings.

Local Storage refers to data storage areas in your browser that websites can use to store information locally on your device. Unlike cookies, this data is not automatically sent to the server with every request.

Similar Technologies include web beacons, pixels, and other tracking technologies that help us understand how our website is used and improve the user experience.

Throughout this policy, we use the term "cookies" to refer to cookies and all similar technologies that we may use on our website.

Types of Cookies We Use

Essential / Strictly Necessary
These cookies are essential for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as security, network management, and accessibility. You cannot opt out of these cookies.
Performance / Analytics
These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting information anonymously. We use this data to improve site performance and user experience. No personal profiling is conducted.
Functionality
These cookies remember choices you make and provide enhanced, personalized features. For example, they may remember your language preferences or theme settings.
Advertising / Marketing
Currently, we do not use advertising or marketing cookies. If this changes in the future, we will update this policy and provide appropriate controls.
Third-Party
These are cookies set by external services we use, such as embedded content from other platforms or analytics services. These third parties have their own cookie policies.

Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways. Please note that removing or blocking cookies may impact your user experience and some functionality may not work as intended.

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings preferences. Here's how to manage cookies in common browsers:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Manage Website Data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data

What Happens When You Block Cookies

Blocking or deleting cookies may result in:

  • Loss of website functionality or features
  • Need to re-enter preferences on each visit
  • Inability to access certain parts of our website
  • Repeated consent requests if you delete preference cookies

We do not control these third-party cookies and are not responsible for their privacy practices. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of any third-party services you use.

Common third-party services we may use: Web analytics providers, hosting services, content delivery networks, and embedded social media content.

Do Not Track & Global Privacy Control

Do Not Track (DNT) is a browser setting that requests websites not to track users. Global Privacy Control (GPC) is a newer standard that signals privacy preferences to websites.

Currently, our website may not automatically respond to DNT or GPC signals. However, we are evaluating these technologies and may implement support for them in the future.

In the meantime, you can manage your privacy preferences using the cookie controls described in this policy and through your browser settings.

Cookie Retention

Different types of cookies are stored for different periods of time:

Session Cookies
These are temporary cookies that are deleted when you close your browser. They are used for essential functionality during your visit.
Persistent Cookies
These cookies remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them. Preference cookies may be stored for up to 12 months to remember your choices.
Analytics Cookies
Performance and analytics cookies may be retained for up to 24 months to help us understand long-term trends and improve our website.

Retention periods may vary depending on the specific cookie type and purpose. You can delete cookies at any time through your browser settings.
