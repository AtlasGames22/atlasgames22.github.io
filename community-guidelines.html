Community Rules

  1. No harassment, hate speech, or discrimination. This includes attacks based on race, gender, religion, sexuality, nationality, or any personal characteristic.
  2. No NSFW, gore, or illegal content. Keep all shared content appropriate for a professional environment and comply with local laws.
  3. No doxxing; protect personal data. Never share someone's private information without consent, including real names, addresses, or contact details.
  4. No spam or self-promotion outside designated channels. Avoid repetitive posting, excessive mentions, or promoting external content without permission.
  5. Use spoiler tags for plot/game content when applicable. Respect others' gaming experience by marking major story reveals or puzzle solutions appropriately.
  6. Report bugs via our bug report system, not general chat. Technical issues should be submitted through our official bug report form for proper tracking.
  7. Follow moderators' instructions. Respect moderation decisions and cooperate with community volunteers who help maintain a positive environment.
  8. Use appropriate channels and threading. Post content in relevant channels and use reply threads to keep discussions organized and accessible.
  9. Be patient and helpful with new community members. Remember everyone was new once, and foster an environment where questions are welcomed and answered kindly.

Moderation Policy

Our moderation approach is progressive and considers context, intent, and severity. We aim to educate and guide community members toward positive behavior rather than immediately resorting to punishment.

Typical Enforcement Progression

  1. Warning: First offense explanation and guidance
  2. Temporary Mute: Brief restriction on posting or speaking
  3. Temporary Ban: Short-term removal from community spaces
  4. Permanent Ban: Complete removal for severe or repeated violations

Severe violations such as harassment, hate speech, or illegal content may result in immediate permanent bans without prior warnings.

Appeals Process

If you believe a moderation action was taken in error, you may appeal by emailing atlasgamesstudio22@gmail.com with the subject line "Moderation Appeal". Include relevant context and be respectful in your communication.

Our Commitment

  • All reports are reviewed promptly, typically within 24 hours
  • We maintain strict confidentiality for reporters
  • Decisions are made fairly based on evidence and context
  • We provide feedback on actions taken when appropriate

Content & Attribution

  • Share only content you have rights to. Don't post copyrighted material without permission from the rights holder.
  • Credit original authors. Always attribute artwork, mods, guides, and other creative content to their creators.
  • Respect licenses for code, art, and music. Understand and follow the terms of any licenses associated with shared content.
  • We may showcase community work with permission. Outstanding community contributions may be featured in our communications with proper credit.
  • Fair use applies to criticism and commentary. You may discuss and critique games and content within reasonable fair use guidelines.
  • When in doubt, ask first. If you're unsure about sharing something, reach out to moderators or the content creator for guidance.

Safety Resources

Your safety and well-being are important to us. In addition to our community moderation, we encourage you to use platform-specific safety tools:

  • Use Discord's built-in blocking and reporting features
  • Adjust privacy settings to control who can contact you
  • Report harmful content directly to platform moderators
  • Take breaks from community spaces when needed
  • Reach out to mental health resources if you're struggling

Emergency Situations

If you're in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, contact local emergency services or crisis hotlines in your area immediately.

Consequences & Enforcement

We believe in proportional responses that match the severity and context of violations. Our goal is always to educate and guide toward positive behavior, but we maintain zero tolerance for severe violations that threaten community safety.

Severe violations including harassment, hate speech, doxxing, or illegal content will result in immediate permanent bans without warnings. We prioritize the safety and well-being of our community members above all else.

Ban evasion through alternate accounts or other means will result in permanent bans for all associated accounts and may involve cooperation with platform administrators.

Changes to These Guidelines

These community guidelines may be updated periodically to reflect the evolving needs of our community and changes in platform policies. We'll announce significant changes in our community spaces and update the revision date below.

We encourage community members to review these guidelines regularly and reach out with questions or suggestions for improvement.
