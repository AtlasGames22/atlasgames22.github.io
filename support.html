Installation & Updates

How do I download and install games?

Visit our Itch.io page and click the download button for your desired game. Extract the ZIP file to a folder on your computer and run the executable file. No additional installation required for most of our games.

How do I check for updates?

Updates are distributed through Itch.io. Re-download the game from your library to get the latest version. We recommend backing up your save files before updating.

Where are my save files located?

Save files are typically stored in your user documents folder under "AtlasGames/[GameName]". You can also check the game's installation directory for a "saves" or "data" folder.

Can I play games offline?

Yes! All our downloadable games work completely offline once installed. Only web-based versions require an internet connection to play.

Crashes & Performance

My game crashes on startup. What should I do?

Try running the game as administrator, update your graphics drivers, and ensure your system meets the minimum requirements. Disable antivirus temporarily to rule out interference.

The game runs slowly or stutters.

Lower the graphics settings in the game options menu. Close other applications to free up system resources. Consider playing in windowed mode if fullscreen causes issues.

Can I change graphics settings?

Most of our games include basic graphics options accessible through the main menu or settings screen. Look for resolution, fullscreen toggle, and quality presets.

How do I report a crash?

Use our bug report form and include your system specifications, what you were doing when the crash occurred, and any error messages that appeared.

Accounts & Purchases

Do I need an account to play?

No account required for most of our games. However, creating an Itch.io account helps you manage your game library and receive updates more easily.

How do I get a refund?

Refunds are handled through the platform where you purchased the game. For Itch.io purchases, contact their support team. See our refund policy for details.

Can I play on multiple devices?

Yes! Once you purchase a game, you can download and install it on any compatible device. Your Itch.io library keeps track of your purchases.

Are there in-game purchases?

Our games focus on complete experiences with no additional purchases required. What you buy is what you get - no microtransactions or DLC.
